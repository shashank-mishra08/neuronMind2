# NeuronMind Content Guidelines
*The voice and tone of the NeuronMind brand.*

## 1. Tone of Voice
*   **Professional & Executive:** Speak directly to C-suite and VP-level decision-makers.
*   **Consulting Tone:** Authoritative, knowledgeable, and grounded in industry realities.
*   **Confident, Not Arrogant:** We understand the problem deeply and have the solution.

## 2. Language Rules
*   **NO Buzzwords:** Avoid empty hype terms.
*   **Banned Words:** "Revolutionary", "Game-changing", "best AI ever", "magic".
*   **Clarity Over Cleverness:** Be direct about what the product does.

## 3. Core Messaging Pillars
*   **The Problem:** Disconnected systems, administrative burden, manual coordination.
*   **The Solution:** An Enterprise Intelligence Layer. AI coordinates, Humans decide.
*   **The Outcome:** Operational Transformation. 

## 4. Storytelling Approach
*   Never dump information. Reveal it progressively.
*   Each section of the website should answer exactly one question and lead naturally into the next.
*   Build understanding -> belief -> trust -> desire -> action.

## 5. Trust & Authenticity
*   No fake metrics, fabricated case studies, or fake logos.
*   Establish trust through deep operational understanding and high-quality design.
